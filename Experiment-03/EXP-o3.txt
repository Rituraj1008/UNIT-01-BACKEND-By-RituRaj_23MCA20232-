Node.js is a powerful and versatile JavaScript runtime that allows developers to build scalable and high-performance applications. Its event-driven, non-blocking I/O model ensures efficient handling of numerous concurrent connections, making it ideal for real-time applications. With a vast ecosystem of packages available through npm, developers can easily integrate various functionalities into their projects. Node.js also benefits from a strong community that continually contributes to its growth and improvement. Its ability to handle both frontend and backend development with JavaScript simplifies the development process and enhances productivity.
